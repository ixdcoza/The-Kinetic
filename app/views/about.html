About Us

We are
The Kinetic

The Kinetic is a motion design studio with a digital and new technology edge. We specialise in creative solutions that tell your story, in the right way, to the right people and using the right platforms, ultimately leading to long term brand equity and commercial success.

Utilising motion design, digital and new technology integration, The Kinetic serves as a conduit for brands and agencies to achieve more integrated and unified creative campaigns.

We specialise in user centred creative, with design, concepts and storytelling at the heart of everything we do.

At the Kinetic we know that delivering a successful creative message requires a holistic approach, and is best accomplished when strategy, creative, and digital are all under one roof and work together. We tailor creative solutions internally and manage the process without outsourcing, keeping costs down, speeding up delivery and keeping the message clear.

Our conceptual thinking considers multiple platform outputs and by utilising screen and digital we deliver story based creative. Our services cover the complete pipeline from conceptualisation, design and art direction, motion and animation, digital integration and of course, deployment.
